about bdwa

Blind Date With an Album was created to help you discover new music using mysterious crowdsourced descriptions. Anyone can contribute a listing by picking a favorite album and describing it in a few short sentences (without revealing the name of the album!). Their listing will appear randomly on the homepage, helping others to discover new music just by browsing for descriptions that catch their eye.

the developers

Blind Date With an Album was created by Victor Chu, Richard Huang, Joe Kerrigan, and Kevin Mo as part of PennApps XXI.



Made with with Django, Python, and not an insignificant amount of coffee.
